Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HoldSafe MM.

What is HoldSafe MM?
HoldSafe MM is an automated escrow middleman service that helps users safely trade cryptocurrencies. Funds are held securely until both parties confirm the deal is complete.
How does creating a deal work?
Join our Discord server and open a deal ticket. Choose the coin, amount, and tag the other party. Our bot guides you through every step from deposit to release.
What coins are supported?
We support Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), Litecoin (LTC), and stablecoins including USDT and USDC across multiple networks.
What happens if something goes wrong?
Our support team is available to mediate disputes. Funds remain locked in escrow until both parties reach an agreement or a resolution is provided by our team.
How fast are deals completed?
Most deals are completed within 15 minutes once both parties fulfill their obligations. Payment detection is instant and confirmations are fast.
